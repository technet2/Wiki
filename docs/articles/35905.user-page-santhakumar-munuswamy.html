About Me

Santhakumar Munuswamy is a C# Corner MVP, Blogger, Author and founder of the blog www.san2debug.net and has around 10 years of experience in Microsoft Technologies.  

I am Santhakumar Munuswamy from Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. I am working as a Technical Lead at Pointel Solutions, Chennai. I have completed Bachelor of Science in 2004 from Govt. Arts College and started my IT career in 2006 with Microsoft Technologies. I have been awarded as C# Corner MVP in 2016.

I have work experience with ASP.NET, C#, WCF, MVC, Entity Framework, Web API, AngularJs, SQL Server, Crystal Reports, PDA, Device Application Development, Web Application Development, Desktop Application and Mobile Application.
