User Page: Carsten Siemens - TechNet Articles - United States (English) - TechNet Wiki

Carsten is a Senior Technical Architect working at Capgemini. He contributes on TechNet Wiki and Forums. Carsten is a moderator of the German Translation Wiki. He also supports Peter Geelen and Noami N in the Community Council's area of focus "Spam, Plagiarism, & Article Deletion". His favorite MS technologies/products are C#, Visual Studio, Azure and SharePoint.
