Luigi Bruno is a software engineer and SharePoint Consultant at MediaSoft in the South East of Italy.

Luigi contributes on TechNet Wiki, Answers Forums, MSDN/TechNet Forums and TechNet "Wiki Ninjas" Blog. He has been a member of the TechNet Wiki Community Council since January 2013; since August 2013, Luigi is the representative member for Italy in theTechNet Wiki International Council.

Luigi is also a moderator for the "Where is the Forum for...?" and for the "Windows 7 Miscellaneous" TechNet Forums, as well as for the MSDN Translation Wiki, where he moderates the Italian language translations of the MSDN Library.
