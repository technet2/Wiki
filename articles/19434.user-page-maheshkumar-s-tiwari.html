Maheshkumar S. Tiwari (Microsoft Integration MVP) is working as a Technical Leader in Pune, India. He has around 8 years of experience with EAI/EDI  technologies like Axway Amtrix and BizTalk. He started working with BizTalk 2009 and now works with BizTalk 2010/2013 and has in-depth knowledge of how BizTalk works. He also gives training to the team members and newcomers on BizTalk and EAI. He loves to share his knowledge, help others and also learn from others as he believes everyone has something which can be learned.
