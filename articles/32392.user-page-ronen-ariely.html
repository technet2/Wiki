Ronen Ariely is a senior consultant in the fields of data bases and applications developing, focusing on Architecture, Projects management, Performance improvements, Troubleshooting, and developing. He has more than 20 years' experience in variety programming languages and technologies, leading development teams, and software companies, BI & SQL Server enterprise level solutions.

Ronen is active in communities in the field of Programming, SQL Server, T-SQL, and he serve as Moderator at MSDN communities (aka pituach), both in the Hebrew interface and in the global interface. Ronen is one of the Microsoft TechNet WIKI communities' leaders.  He is the founder of the Microsoft TechNet WIKI Ninjas groups on Facebook, and serve as co-admin at the global group and several local groups (Persian, Turkish, Spanish, etc'). Ronen writes technical blogs and TechNet articles in the field of SQL Server and .Net.

Forums

Ronen passionate to support others reflected most of all in his activities in forums. Ronen's first activities in forums as Moderator started at 1999, at 2001 he finish developing, his first forum interface from scratch (using classic asp which was converted to ASP.Net later). WikiNinjas - Microsoft Official Facebook groups

Ronen is the founder of the Microsoft TechNet WIKI groups on Facebook, and Serves as admin at the global group as well as at several local groups. The first idea of creating Facebook groups for the Microsoft TechNet WIKI was raised by Ronen Ariely (aka pituach) on several occasions till he started an official discussion regarding the issue with the title “Should there be a page or group on Facebook for Wiki Ninjas”. Ed Price, Peter Geelen, Saeid Hasani, and Naomi, supported the idea and Facebook Groups were created with this first step.

Today Ronen serves as Admin at the global group (English language), and several Local groups like the Turkish, Persian , and Spanish languages.
