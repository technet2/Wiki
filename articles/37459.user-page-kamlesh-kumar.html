Introduction

Kamlesh Kumar is a Technical lead in the fields of BizTalk Server and Integration. He plays a lead role in integration solution and provide advice to clients about integration. He has more than 9 years experience in variety programming languages and technologies, leading development teams, and software companies.

You can find Kamlesh actively participating on BizTalk forum giving answer to challenging questions and participating in interesting discussion. Apart from MSDN forum he also be found posting articles on TechNet Wiki. Kamlesh writes technical blogs and TechNet articles in the field of BizTalk Server and Azure Logic Apps. Kamlesh is one of the most active member on Microsoft TechNet WIKI communities and WIKI Ninja Facebook group.
