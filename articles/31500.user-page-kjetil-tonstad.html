Introduction

Kjetil has experience with integration since 2006, and is currently working with Big Data and Data Platform technologies in Microsoft Azure. Have also been performing advisory tasks, architectural design, BizTalk/BizTalk360 installations and configuration, and BizTalk health checks. Certified expert on all available BizTalk versions since 2006, and holds MCITP, MCSA, MCDST, MCTS, MCPS and ITIL certifications. Kjetil has participated in Microsoft Technology Adoption Programs, and was awarded Most Valuable Contributor for BizTalk Server 2010. Microsoft has also benchmarked one of his business critical enterprise environments as top 10% in the EMEA region. Awarded BizTalk360 Product Specialist. Always interested in learning new technology, and recently completed the Microsoft Professional Program in Big Data and Data Science. Educated Master of Information Technology, with specialization in system development and project management.
