Visakh Murukesan aka Visakh16 is a Business Intelligence Architect based in India who has over 12 years of experience in designing and implementing end to end datawarehouse projects using Microsoft BI Stack consisting of SSIS,SSRS,SSAS with SQLServer as the backend database. Visakh is very active in SQLServer Technical Communities as a blogger, Wiki author/editor and in forums. He has also done some speaking engagements in SQLSaturday Dallas. He has been awarded the Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) award (2010) for his contributions to the SQLServer Tech community. He has been re-awarded in 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018.
